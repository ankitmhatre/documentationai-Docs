<Update label="2024-12-23" description="v0.3.35" tags={["feature", "major"]}>

## Major Release: Nutrition Management Platform

Snacko has evolved from a simple nutrition logging app into a comprehensive nutrition management platform!

### For Dietitians & Coaches

**Client Management**
- Invite and manage clients individually or in bulk via CSV upload
- View comprehensive client profiles with detailed statistics
- Track real-time adherence with percentage monitoring
- Monitor client progress across multiple metrics

**Custom Diet Plan Creation**
- Create personalized diet plans tailored to each client's needs
- Set custom meal slots with specific foods and portions
- Configure diet goals, medical conditions, allergies, and cuisine preferences
- Track nutrition metrics including calories, macros, and more

**Analytics & Insights**
- View organization-wide adherence graphs
- Monitor average adherence across all clients
- Track calorie consumption trends
- Identify top deviating clients for focused attention

**Organization Support**
- Create and manage organizations
- Invite team members and clients
- Organize clients by organization for better management

### For Clients

**Enhanced Experience**
- View assigned diet plans from your nutritionist
- Track your daily nutrition intake
- Monitor adherence to your personalized plan
- Access detailed progress statistics

### Authentication & Security

- **OTP Verification**: Secure one-time password verification for account setup
- **Apple Sign-In**: Improved Apple Sign-In support with better error handling
- **Enhanced Security**: Better token management and authentication flow

### User Experience Improvements

- New welcome screens for first-time users
- Improved navigation and screen transitions
- Better empty states and loading indicators
- Enhanced feedback and error messaging

### Bug Fixes & Improvements

- Fixed Apple Sign-In authentication issues
- Improved error handling and user feedback
- Enhanced app stability and performance
- Better handling of edge cases in diet plan management

</Update>

## How to Update

Follow these steps to ensure you have the latest version.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for Updates" icon="download">
    Open the Snacko app and navigate to Settings > About.
    
    Look for the "Check for Updates" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install" icon="package">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
        Tap Update in the App Store notification.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
        Use Google Play Store to download the latest APK.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart App" icon="refresh-cw">
    Force close and reopen Snacko to apply all changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
