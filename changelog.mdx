<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.3.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Personalized weekly meal plans generated using your behavior data and preferences.
- Real-time nutrition scanning via camera for effortless logging.

## Improvements

- Sync speed increased by `>50%` for multi-device usage.
- Dashboard now shows predictive goal progress based on habits.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed inaccurate calorie estimates for custom recipes.
- Resolved login issues on shared networks.

## How to Update

Follow these steps to ensure you have the latest version.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for Updates" icon="download">
    Open the Snacko app and navigate to Settings > About.
    
    Look for the "Check for Updates" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install" icon="package">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
        Tap Update in the App Store notification.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
        Use Google Play Store to download the latest APK.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart App" icon="refresh-cw">
    Force close and reopen Snacko to apply all changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
