## Overview

Snacko is a powerful nutrition management platform designed for nutrition coaches and their clients. Whether you're a professional dietitian managing multiple clients or an individual tracking your personal nutrition goals, Snacko provides the tools you need to create, assign, and follow personalized diet plans with ease.

## Who Snacko Is For

Snacko serves nutrition professionals and individuals seeking better nutrition management.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Nutrition Coaches" icon="users">
    **For Dietitians and Nutrition Coaches:**
    
    - Create and customize diet plans for each client
    - Add clients individually or in bulk via CSV upload
    - Assign diet plans with specific meal slots and food items
    - Track client adherence and monitor progress
    - View organization-wide analytics and insights
    - Manage multiple clients efficiently
    
    Snacko helps you scale your nutrition coaching practice while maintaining personalized attention for each client.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Clients" icon="user">
    **For Clients and Individuals:**
    
    - View diet plans assigned by your nutrition coach
    - Log meals and track daily nutrition intake
    - Monitor adherence to your personalized plan
    - Add food items to meal slots
    - Track progress with detailed statistics
    - Use Snacko personally for your own nutrition goals
    
    Whether you're working with a coach or managing your own nutrition, Snacko makes it easy to stay on track.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Quick Start

Get started with Snacko in just a few steps.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Coaches" icon="users">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
        Download the Snacko mobile app or visit the web dashboard and create your coach account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create Organization" icon="building">
        Set up your organization to start managing clients and diet plans.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Clients" icon="user-plus">
        Invite clients via email or phone number, or upload a CSV file for bulk imports.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create Diet Plan" icon="book-open">
        Create your first personalized diet plan with meal slots and food items.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Assign to Client" icon="send">
        Assign the diet plan to a client and start tracking their adherence.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Clients" icon="user">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
        Download the Snacko mobile app and create your account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Accept Invitation" icon="mail">
        Accept the invitation from your nutrition coach if you're working with one.
      </Step>
      <Step title="View Your Plan" icon="eye">
        Review your assigned diet plan with meal slots and food recommendations.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log Your Meals" icon="utensils">
        Start logging meals and track your daily nutrition intake.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Monitor Progress" icon="bar-chart-3">
        Check your adherence and progress statistics regularly.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
