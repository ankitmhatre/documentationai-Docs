## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address
- Access to a modern web browser or the Snacko mobile app
- Your basic health information ready (age, weight, height, activity level)

</Callout>

Snacko makes it easy to start tracking your nutrition. Follow these steps to create your account and log your first meal in under 5 minutes.

## Create Your Account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="globe">
    Visit [https://app.example.com](https://app.example.com) and click **Sign Up**.

    ```bash
    # No code needed - just use the web form
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Download Snacko from the App Store and tap **Get Started**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Install Snacko from Google Play and select **Create Account**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree to Terms" icon="file-text">
    Review and accept the terms of service and privacy policy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Complete Profile Setup

Fill out your profile to personalize your nutrition plans. Snacko uses this data to recommend goals based on behavior science.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Health Info" icon="activity">
    Enter your age, gender, current weight (`<70kg`), height (`>170cm`), and activity level (sedentary, moderate, active).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Dietary Preferences" icon="settings">
    Select preferences like vegetarian, allergies (nuts, dairy), or goals (weight loss, muscle gain).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Initial Nutrition Goals

Define clear, achievable targets to guide your tracking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Goal Type" icon="target">
    Pick from weight loss (`>2kg/month`), maintenance, or gain. Snacko suggests daily calories based on your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Macros" icon="pie-chart">
    Adjust macros if needed: carbs `45-65%`, protein `10-35%`, fats `20-35%`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Goals" icon="check-circle">
    Review summary and save. Your dashboard now shows progress trackers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log Your First Meal

Track intake effortlessly to build habits.

  src="https://via.placeholder.com/800x400/cccccc/ffffff?text=Log+Meal+Screenshot"

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search Food" icon="search">
    Tap **+ Log Meal** and search for "grilled chicken salad" (200g chicken, mixed greens, olive oil dressing).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details" icon="edit">
    Input quantity and confirm nutrients: `500kcal`, `40g protein`, `20g fat`, `30g carbs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Save" icon="save">
    See instant feedback on goal alignment and save. View daily summary on dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Pro tip: Use the barcode scanner for packaged foods or voice input for quick logging on the go.

</Callout>
