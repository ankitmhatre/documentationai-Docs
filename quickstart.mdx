## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address or phone number
- Access to the Snacko mobile app (iOS or Android)
- Basic information about yourself or your clients (for coaches)

</Callout>

Snacko makes it easy to get started. Follow these steps based on whether you're a nutrition coach or a client.

## Getting Started

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Coaches" icon="users">
    ### Step 1: Create Your Account

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download the App" icon="smartphone">
        Download Snacko from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
        Create your account using email, phone number, or Apple Sign-In.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify Your Account" icon="shield">
        Complete OTP verification sent to your email or phone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Coach Mode" icon="users">
        When prompted, select **Coach Mode** to access coach features.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 2: Create Your Organization

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Organization" icon="building">
        Set up your organization with a name (e.g., "My Nutrition Practice").
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set as Default" icon="star">
        Mark this organization as your default if you have multiple organizations.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 3: Add Your First Client

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Client" icon="user-plus">
        Go to **Clients** tab â†’ Tap **+ Add Client**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Client Details" icon="edit">
        Enter client's email or phone number, and optionally their first and last name.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send Invitation" icon="send">
        Client will receive an invitation link to join your organization.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 4: Create Your First Diet Plan

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Client" icon="user">
        Go to the client's profile and tap **Create Diet Plan**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set Plan Details" icon="settings">
        Enter plan name, start date, end date, and select diet goals.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Meal Slots" icon="clock">
        Create meal slots (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks) with times.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Food Items" icon="search">
        Search and add food items to each meal slot with quantities.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save Plan" icon="save">
        Save the plan and it will be assigned to your client automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 5: Track Adherence

    <Steps>
      <Step title="View Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
        Check the **Home** tab to see organization-wide adherence statistics.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Monitor Clients" icon="eye">
        View individual client adherence percentages and identify who needs support.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="tip">
      You can also bulk import clients via CSV file for faster onboarding. Check the "Manage Clients" guide for details.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Clients" icon="user">
    ### Step 1: Create Your Account

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download the App" icon="smartphone">
        Download Snacko from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
        Create your account using email, phone number, or Apple Sign-In.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify Your Account" icon="shield">
        Complete OTP verification sent to your email or phone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Client Mode" icon="user">
        When prompted, select **Client Mode** to access client features.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 2: Accept Coach Invitation (If Applicable)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check Your Email/Phone" icon="mail">
        If your coach invited you, you'll receive an invitation link.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Accept Invitation" icon="check">
        Tap the invitation link or enter the invite code in the app.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Join Organization" icon="users">
        You'll be added to your coach's organization automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 3: View Your Assigned Diet Plan

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Home" icon="home">
        On the **Home** tab, you'll see your active diet plan if one is assigned.
      </Step>
      <Step title="View Meal Slots" icon="eye">
        Tap on the diet plan to see all meal slots with recommended foods.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Meal Times" icon="clock">
        Review meal times and food quantities for each meal slot.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 4: Log Your First Meal

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Meal Slot" icon="utensils">
        Tap on a meal slot (e.g., "Breakfast") to view assigned foods.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Mark as Consumed" icon="check">
        Tap the checkmark next to foods you've eaten to log them.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Additional Foods" icon="plus">
        If needed, you can add extra food items to meal slots.
      </Step>
      <Step title="View Adherence" icon="bar-chart-3">
        Check your adherence percentage to see how well you're following the plan.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Step 5: Track Your Progress

    <Steps>
      <Step title="View Statistics" icon="trending-up">
        Check the **Stats** tab to see your nutrition summary and progress.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Monitor Adherence" icon="target">
        Track your daily adherence percentage and see trends over time.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="tip">
      You can also use Snacko personally without a coach by creating your own diet plans. Check the "Using Snacko Personally" guide for details.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
