## Add a Meal

1. Tap the **+** icon on the home screen
2. Select "Add Meal"
3. Search your food or scan the barcode
4. Enter the portion size and time
5. Tap **Save**

## Tips

- You can edit meals later from your History
- Custom foods can be added if not found
- Meal tracking helps optimize your plan
