## Overview

Snacko isn't just for working with nutrition coachesâ€”you can also use it personally to manage your own nutrition goals. Create your own diet plans, track meals, and monitor progress independently.

## Creating Your Personal Diet Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Plans" icon="folder">
    Navigate to the **Plans** or **Diet Plans** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Plan" icon="plus">
    Tap **+ Create Plan** or **New Diet Plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Plan Details" icon="settings">
    Enter:
    - Plan name (e.g., "My Weight Loss Plan")
    - Start date and end date
    - Diet goals (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Preferences" icon="heart">
    Set:
    - Meal types you prefer
    - Allergies or dietary restrictions
    - Cuisine preferences
    - Eating preferences (vegetarian, keto, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Meal Slots" icon="clock">
    Create meal slots:
    - Name each meal (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks)
    - Set meal times
    - Add descriptions if needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Food Items" icon="search">
    Search and add food items to each meal slot:
    - Use Snacko's food database
    - Set quantities and serving sizes
    - Balance nutrition across meals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Nutrition Goals" icon="target">
    Configure target metrics:
    - Daily calories
    - Protein, carbs, and fat targets
    - Other metrics you want to track
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Plan" icon="save">
    Save your personal diet plan and start following it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Personal Nutrition Goals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Your Goals" icon="target">
    Determine what you want to achieve:
    - Weight loss
    - Weight gain
    - Muscle building
    - Maintenance
    - Improved energy
    - Better nutrition
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calculate Targets" icon="calculator">
    Set realistic targets:
    - Daily calorie goals
    - Macronutrient ratios
    - Meal timing preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Plan Accordingly" icon="book-open">
    Build your diet plan to align with your goals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking Your Personal Progress

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log Daily Meals" icon="utensils">
    Log all meals and snacks you consume:
    - Mark foods from your plan as consumed
    - Add any additional foods you eat
    - Use accurate quantities
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Adherence" icon="trending-up">
    Track how well you're following your plan:
    - View adherence percentage
    - See which meals you're missing
    - Identify patterns
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Nutrition Stats" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Check your nutrition summary:
    - Daily totals (calories, macros)
    - Weekly averages
    - Progress toward goals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust as Needed" icon="edit">
    Modify your plan based on:
    - What's working
    - What's challenging
    - Progress toward goals
    - Lifestyle changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Between Coach Plans and Personal Plans

You can have both coach-assigned plans and personal plans:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View All Plans" icon="folder">
    Go to the **Plans** tab to see all your plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Active Plan" icon="click">
    Choose which plan you want to follow:
    - Coach-assigned plans (if you have a coach)
    - Personal plans you created
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch Plans" icon="refresh">
    You can switch between plans as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Separately" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Each plan has its own adherence and progress tracking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
If you have a coach, you can use personal plans for days when you want to follow your own meal plan, while still maintaining your coach relationship.
</Callout>

## Customizing Your Personal Plan

### Edit Meal Slots

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Plan" icon="folder">

  </Step>

</Steps>

### Update Food Items

  <Step title="Modify Foods" icon="edit">

    - Add variety
    - Balance nutrition
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adjust Goals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Update Targets" icon="sliders">

    - Change calorie targets
    - Adjust macro ratios

</Steps>

## Benefits of Personal Plans

**Why Use Personal Plans:**
- **Independence**: Manage your nutrition on your own terms
- **Flexibility**: Create plans that fit your unique lifestyle
- **Learning**: Understand your eating patterns better
- **Experimentation**: Try different approaches to nutrition
- **Control**: Full control over your meal planning

## Tips for Personal Use

<Expandable title="Best Practices" default-open="false">
- **Start with Goals**: Define clear nutrition goals before creating your plan
- **Be Honest**: Log everything accurately for meaningful insights
- **Stay Flexible**: Adjust your plan as you learn what works
- **Balance Nutrition**: Ensure your plan includes all essential nutrients
- **Plan Ahead**: Create meal slots that align with your daily schedule
- **Track Progress**: Regularly review your adherence and nutrition stats
- **Celebrate Wins**: Acknowledge when you meet your goals
- **Learn from Data**: Use tracking data to understand your eating patterns
</Expandable>

## When to Work with a Coach

Consider working with a nutrition coach if:
- You want professional guidance
- You have specific health conditions
- You need accountability
- You want expert meal planning
- You're struggling to meet goals on your own

You can use both personal plans and coach-assigned plans simultaneously.
