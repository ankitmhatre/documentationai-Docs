## Overview

Creating a diet plan in Snacko allows you to customize meal slots, add specific food items, and set nutrition goals for each client. This guide walks you through the entire process.

<Callout kind="info">
Diet plans can be created for specific date ranges and include multiple meal slots with detailed food recommendations.
</Callout>

## Step 1: Navigate to Create Diet Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Client" icon="user">
    Go to the **Clients** tab and select the client you want to create a diet plan for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Plan" icon="plus">
    Tap **Create Diet Plan** or **+ New Plan** button on the client's profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Set Basic Plan Information

Fill in the essential details for your diet plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan Name" icon="edit">
    Enter a descriptive name (e.g., "Weight Loss Plan - January 2024"). If left empty, it will be auto-generated from the client's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Date" icon="calendar">
    Select when the diet plan should begin. This is the first day the client should start following the plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="End Date" icon="calendar">
    Select when the diet plan should end. The plan will remain active until this date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status" icon="toggle">
    Set the plan status:
    - **Active**: Client should follow this plan
    - **Inactive**: Plan is saved but not currently active
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Configure Diet Goals and Preferences

Customize the plan based on your client's needs:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Diet Goals" icon="target">
    Select one or more diet goals:
    - Weight Loss
    - Weight Gain
    - Muscle Gain
    - Maintenance
    - Medical Condition Management
    - Performance Enhancement
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meal Types" icon="utensils">
    Specify meal types to include (e.g., Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Medical Conditions" icon="heart">
    Add any relevant medical conditions (e.g., Diabetes, Hypertension, PCOS).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allergies" icon="alert-triangle">
    List food allergies or intolerances (e.g., Nuts, Dairy, Gluten).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cuisine Preferences" icon="globe">
    Select preferred cuisines (e.g., Mediterranean, Asian, Indian, Western).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Eating Preferences" icon="settings">
    Set preferences like:
    - Vegetarian/Vegan
    - High Protein
    - Low Carb
    - Keto
    - Intermittent Fasting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Add Meal Slots

Create meal slots with specific times and descriptions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Meal Slot" icon="plus">
    Tap **+ Add Meal Slot** to create a new meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the Meal" icon="edit">
    Enter a name (e.g., "Breakfast", "Morning Snack", "Lunch", "Dinner").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Time" icon="clock">
    Select the recommended time for this meal (e.g., 8:00 AM for breakfast).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Description" icon="file-text">
    Optionally add notes or instructions for this meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repeat for All Meals" icon="repeat">
    Add all meal slots your client should follow throughout the day.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
You can create meal slots for specific days of the week or use the same slots for all days. Consider your client's schedule when setting meal times.
</Callout>

## Step 5: Add Food Items to Meal Slots

Populate each meal slot with specific food items:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Meal Slot" icon="click">
    Tap on a meal slot to open it and add foods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Food Database" icon="search">
    Use the search bar to find food items from Snacko's comprehensive database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Food Item" icon="check">
    Tap on a food item to add it to the meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Specify the quantity and serving size (e.g., 100g, 1 cup, 2 pieces).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Multiple Foods" icon="plus">
    Continue adding food items until the meal slot is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Food Item Options:**
- Search by name, brand, or barcode
- View nutrition information (calories, protein, carbs, fats)
- Select appropriate serving sizes
- Add custom foods if not found in database

## Step 6: Set Nutrition Metrics (Optional)

Configure target metrics to track client progress:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Metric" icon="target">
    Add metrics like:
    - Daily Calories
    - Protein (grams)
    - Carbohydrates (grams)
    - Fats (grams)
    - Weight
    - Body Fat Percentage
    - Custom metrics
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Target Values" icon="sliders">
    Define target values for each metric (e.g., 2000 calories, 150g protein).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 7: Add Notes (Optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add General Notes" icon="file-text">
    Include any additional instructions, tips, or information for the client.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 8: Review and Save

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Plan" icon="eye">
    Review all meal slots, food items, and settings before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Plan" icon="save">
    Tap **Save** or **Create Plan** to finalize the diet plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Assigned" icon="check">
    The plan will be automatically assigned to your client and they'll be able to see it in their app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing an Existing Diet Plan

You can edit diet plans after creation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plan" icon="folder">
    Go to the client's profile and select the diet plan you want to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Details" icon="edit">
    Tap **Edit** to modify plan details, meal slots, or food items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Save your changes and they'll be reflected in the client's view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Creating Effective Diet Plans

<Expandable title="Best Practices" default-open="false">
- **Start Simple**: Begin with basic meal slots and gradually add complexity
- **Consider Schedule**: Set meal times that align with your client's daily routine
- **Balance Nutrition**: Ensure each meal slot includes a good balance of macros
- **Be Specific**: Include exact quantities to make tracking easier for clients
- **Add Variety**: Include different foods to prevent boredom and ensure nutritional diversity
- **Set Realistic Goals**: Make sure targets are achievable for your client
- **Use Templates**: Save common plans as templates for faster creation
</Expandable>
