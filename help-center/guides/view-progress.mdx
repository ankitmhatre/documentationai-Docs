## Overview

Snacko provides comprehensive progress tracking to help you monitor your nutrition intake, adherence to your diet plan, and overall progress toward your goals. This guide shows you how to access and understand all available statistics.

<Callout kind="info">
Progress data updates in real-time as you log meals. Check your stats regularly to stay on track.
</Callout>

## Accessing Your Statistics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Stats Tab" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Navigate to the **Stats** or **Progress** tab in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Overview" icon="eye">
    See your overall statistics and progress summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Daily Nutrition Summary

View your nutrition intake for today:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Today" icon="calendar">
    Make sure you're viewing today's date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Totals" icon="list">
    See:
    - **Total Calories**: Calories consumed today
    - **Protein**: Grams of protein
    - **Carbohydrates**: Grams of carbs
    - **Fats**: Grams of fats
    - **Other Nutrients**: Fiber, vitamins, minerals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare to Goals" icon="target">
    See how your intake compares to your plan targets:
    - Percentage of goal met
    - Remaining targets
    - Over/under indicators
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adherence Tracking

Monitor how well you're following your assigned diet plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Adherence Percentage" icon="percent">
    See your overall adherence:
    - Daily adherence percentage
    - What percentage of assigned foods you've logged
    - Adherence trends over time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Meal Slot Adherence" icon="list">
    See adherence for each meal slot:
    - Which meals you've completed
    - Which meals are still pending
    - Which foods you haven't logged yet
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Adherence Graph" icon="trending-up">

    - Daily adherence over time
    - Weekly averages
    - Monthly trends
    - Patterns and consistency
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Weekly Progress

View your progress over the past week:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Weekly View" icon="calendar">

  </Step>
  <Step title="View Weekly Summary" icon="bar-chart-3">
    See:
    - Average daily calories
    - Average macros (protein, carbs, fats)
    - Weekly adherence average
    - Day-by-day breakdown
    - Best and worst days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare Weeks" icon="compare">
    Compare this week to previous weeks:
    - Progress trends
    - Improvements or declines
    - Consistency patterns
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Progress Charts and Graphs

**Available Charts:**

## Understanding Your Data

### High Adherence (90%+)
- You're following your plan very well
- Continue with current approach
- Consider if you can maintain this long-term

### Moderate Adherence (70-89%)
- You're mostly following the plan
- Identify areas for improvement
- Small adjustments may help

### Low Adherence (Below 70%)
- You may be struggling with the plan
- Review what's challenging
- Consider adjusting your plan
- Reach out to your coach if you have one

## Tips for Using Progress Data

<Expandable title="Best Practices" default-open="false">
- **Check Daily**: Review your stats daily to stay aware
- **Look for Patterns**: Identify trends over time, not just daily fluctuations
- **Focus on Consistency**: Consistent adherence is more important than perfect days
- **Use Data to Adjust**: Modify your plan based on what the data shows
- **Celebrate Progress**: Acknowledge improvements and milestones
- **Don't Obsess**: Use data as a guide, not a source of stress
- **Share with Coach**: If you have a coach, share insights for better guidance
</Expandable>
