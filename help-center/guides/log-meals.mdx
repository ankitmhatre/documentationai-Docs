## Overview

Logging meals in Snacko helps you track your daily nutrition intake and monitor adherence to your assigned diet plan. This guide shows you how to log meals, mark foods as consumed, and track your progress.

<Callout kind="info">
You can log meals by marking assigned foods as consumed or by adding additional foods you eat throughout the day.
</Callout>

## Viewing Your Assigned Diet Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Home" icon="home">
    Open the **Home** tab in the Snacko app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Active Plan" icon="eye">
    See your active diet plan with all meal slots for today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Meal Slot" icon="click">
    Tap on a meal slot (e.g., "Breakfast", "Lunch") to see assigned foods.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging Meals: Mark Foods as Consumed

The easiest way to log meals is to mark assigned foods as consumed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Meal Slot" icon="folder">
    Tap on a meal slot to view assigned foods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Assigned Foods" icon="list">
    See all food items assigned for this meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as Consumed" icon="check">
    Tap the checkmark or "Consumed" button next to foods you've eaten.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Quantity" icon="sliders">
    If you ate a different quantity, adjust it before marking as consumed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See Updated Adherence" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Your adherence percentage updates automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding Additional Foods

If you eat foods not in your assigned plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Meal Slot" icon="folder">
    Go to the meal slot where you want to add food.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add Food" icon="plus">
    Tap **+ Add Food** or **Add Item** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Food" icon="search">
    Search Snacko's food database:
    - Type food name
    - Scan barcode for packaged foods
    - Browse categories
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Food" icon="check">
    Tap on the food item you want to add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Enter the quantity and serving size you consumed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save" icon="save">
    The food is added to your meal slot and tracked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Adding all foods you eat, even if not in the plan, helps you get a complete picture of your nutrition intake.
</Callout>

## Using Barcode Scanner

For packaged foods, use the barcode scanner:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Scanner" icon="camera">
    When adding food, tap the barcode scanner icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan Barcode" icon="scan">
    Point your camera at the product's barcode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Fill Details" icon="magic">
    Food information is automatically populated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Set the quantity you consumed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Meal" icon="save">
    Add the food to your meal slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Your Progress

### Daily Summary

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Stats Tab" icon="bar-chart-3">

  </Step>
  <Step title="View Daily Summary" icon="calendar">

    - Total calories consumed
    - Protein, carbs, and fats
    - Adherence percentage
    - Meals logged
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adherence Tracking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Adherence" icon="target">

## Editing Logged Meals

If you need to correct a logged meal:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Meal Slot" icon="folder">
    Go to the meal slot with the logged food.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Food" icon="click">
    Tap on the logged food item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Details" icon="edit">
    Modify:
    - Quantity consumed
    - Mark as not consumed
    - Remove from log
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Your adherence updates automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <Step title="View Past Days" icon="arrow-left">

## Nutrition Information

When you log foods, you can see:

**Nutrition Details:**
- Calories
- Protein (grams)
- Carbohydrates (grams)
- Fats (grams)
- Other nutrients (fiber, vitamins, minerals)

**Meal Totals:**
- Total nutrition for each meal slot
- Daily totals across all meals
- Comparison to your plan targets

## Tips for Effective Meal Logging

<Expandable title="Best Practices" default-open="false">
- **Log Immediately**: Log meals right after eating for accuracy
- **Be Honest**: Log everything you eat, even if it's not in the plan
- **Use Accurate Quantities**: Measure or estimate portions carefully
- **Use Barcode Scanner**: Fastest way to log packaged foods
- **Check Regularly**: Review your adherence and progress daily
- **Don't Skip Meals**: Log all meals, even if you miss assigned foods
- **Ask Questions**: Contact your coach if you're unsure about logging
- **Stay Consistent**: Regular logging gives better insights
</Expandable>

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Find a Food Item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Try Different Search Terms" icon="search">
    Use alternative names or search by brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Barcode Scanner" icon="camera">
    Scan the barcode if it's a packaged food.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Custom Food" icon="plus">
    If not found, you can add it as a custom food item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adherence Not Updating

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh App" icon="refresh">
    Pull down to refresh the home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection" icon="wifi">
    Ensure you have an active internet connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait a Moment" icon="clock">
    Adherence updates may take a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
