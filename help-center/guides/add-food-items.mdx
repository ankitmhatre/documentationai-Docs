## Overview

Adding food items to meal slots is a core feature of Snacko. Coaches can add foods when creating diet plans, and clients can add additional foods to their assigned meal slots. This guide covers both scenarios.

<Callout kind="info">
Snacko includes a comprehensive food database. You can search by name, brand, or barcode to find food items.
</Callout>

## For Coaches: Adding Foods to Diet Plans

### Step 1: Open Meal Slot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or Edit Plan" icon="book-open">
    Create a new diet plan or edit an existing one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Meal Slot" icon="click">
    Tap on a meal slot (e.g., "Breakfast", "Lunch") to open it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Search for Food Items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Add Food" icon="plus">
    Tap **+ Add Food** or **Search Foods** button in the meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Database" icon="search">
    Use the search bar to find food items:
    - Type food name (e.g., "chicken breast", "brown rice")
    - Search by brand name
    - Scan barcode for packaged foods
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Results" icon="list">
    Browse search results and view nutrition information for each item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Select and Configure Food Item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Food" icon="check">
    Tap on a food item from the search results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Nutrition Info" icon="info">
    Review nutrition details:
    - Calories
    - Protein (grams)
    - Carbohydrates (grams)
    - Fats (grams)
    - Other nutrients
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Specify the quantity:
    - Enter amount (e.g., 100g, 1 cup, 2 pieces)
    - Select serving size from available options
    - Nutrition values update automatically
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Meal Slot" icon="save">
    Tap **Add** to add the food item to the meal slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 4: Add Multiple Foods

<Steps>
  <Step title="Repeat Process" icon="repeat">
    Continue adding food items until the meal slot is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Total Nutrition" icon="bar-chart-3">
    See the total nutrition summary for all foods in the meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Balance Meal" icon="scale">
    Ensure the meal slot has a good balance of macros and nutrients.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Clients: Adding Foods to Assigned Plans

Clients can add additional food items to their assigned meal slots:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assigned Plan" icon="folder">
    Go to **Home** tab and tap on your active diet plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Meal Slot" icon="click">
    Tap on a meal slot (e.g., "Breakfast") to view assigned foods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Food" icon="plus">
    Tap **+ Add Food** or **Add Item** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and Select" icon="search">
    Search for food items and select the ones you want to add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Specify the quantity and serving size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save" icon="save">
    The food item is added to your meal slot and tracked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Clients can add foods to track everything they eat, even if it's not in the original plan. This helps with accurate adherence tracking.
</Callout>

## Creating Custom Meal Slots

### For Coaches

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Meal Slot" icon="plus">
    When creating or editing a diet plan, tap **+ Add Meal Slot**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the Slot" icon="edit">
    Enter a name (e.g., "Pre-Workout Snack", "Evening Tea").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Time" icon="clock">
    Select the recommended time for this meal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Description" icon="file-text">
    Optionally add notes or instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Foods" icon="search">
    Add food items to this meal slot as described above.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### For Clients

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Personal Meal Slot" icon="plus">
    In your diet plan view, tap **+ Add Meal Slot**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Slot" icon="settings">
    Name the slot, set time, and add foods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save" icon="save">
    The meal slot is added to your plan for tracking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Food Items in Meal Slots

### Edit Food Quantities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Meal Slot" icon="folder">
    Open the meal slot containing the food item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Food" icon="click">
    Tap on the food item you want to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Adjust the quantity or serving size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Save the updated quantity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Remove Food Items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Meal Slot" icon="folder">
    Open the meal slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Food" icon="trash">
    Swipe left on the food item or tap the delete/remove button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Removal" icon="check">
    Confirm to remove the food item from the meal slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Barcode Scanner

For packaged foods:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Scanner" icon="camera">
    Tap the barcode scanner icon when adding foods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan Barcode" icon="scan">
    Point your camera at the product's barcode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Fill Information" icon="magic">
    Food details are automatically populated from the database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Quantity" icon="sliders">
    Adjust quantity if needed and add to meal slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Food Database Features

**What's Available:**
- Comprehensive food database with thousands of items
- Brand name products
- Generic foods and ingredients
- Restaurant menu items
- Custom foods (if not found in database)

**Search Tips:**
- Use specific names for better results (e.g., "grilled chicken breast" vs "chicken")
- Search by brand when looking for packaged foods
- Use barcode scanner for fastest results
- Browse categories if unsure of exact name

## Nutrition Tracking

When you add foods to meal slots:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automatic Calculation" icon="calculator">
    Nutrition values (calories, macros) are calculated automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Totals" icon="bar-chart-3">
    See total nutrition for each meal slot and daily totals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare to Goals" icon="target">
    Compare actual intake to plan goals and targets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Adding Food Items

<Expandable title="Best Practices" default-open="false">
- **Be Specific**: Use exact quantities for accurate tracking
- **Check Serving Sizes**: Verify serving sizes match what you're actually consuming
- **Balance Meals**: Ensure each meal slot has a good mix of nutrients
- **Use Barcode Scanner**: Fastest way to add packaged foods
- **Add Everything**: Include all foods and beverages for complete tracking
- **Update Quantities**: Adjust quantities if portions differ from the plan
- **Review Nutrition**: Check that meal slots meet your nutrition goals
</Expandable>
