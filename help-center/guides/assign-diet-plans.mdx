description: Guide for nutrition coaches to assign diet plans to clients for specific dates.
---

## Overview

Assigning diet plans allows you to schedule when clients should follow specific meal plans. You can assign plans for individual days or date ranges, and clients will see these assignments in their app.

<Callout kind="info">
Diet plans can be assigned to clients immediately when created, or you can assign existing plans to specific dates later.
</Callout>

## Automatic Assignment

When you create a new diet plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Plan" icon="book-open">
    Create a diet plan for a client (see "Create Diet Plan" guide).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Date Range" icon="calendar">
    Specify the start date and end date for the plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Auto-Assigned" icon="check">
    The plan is automatically assigned to the client for the specified date range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client Notification" icon="bell">
    The client will see the assigned plan in their app starting from the start date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manual Assignment for Specific Dates

You can also assign existing diet plans to specific dates:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Client" icon="user">
    Go to the client's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Diet Plans" icon="folder">
    Tap on **Diet Plans** to see all available plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Plan" icon="click">
    Choose the diet plan you want to assign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to Date" icon="calendar">
    Tap **Assign** and select a specific date or date range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Assignment" icon="check">
    Confirm the assignment. The client will see this plan for the selected dates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning Plans with Meal Slots

When assigning a diet plan, you can customize meal slots for specific days:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Date" icon="calendar">
    Choose the date you want to assign the plan to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Day" icon="calendar">
    Specify the day name (e.g., Monday, Tuesday).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Meal Slots" icon="utensils">
    Set up meal slots for this specific day:
    - Meal slot names (e.g., "Breakfast", "Lunch")
    - Food items for each slot
    - Quantities and serving sizes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Assignment" icon="save">
    Save the assignment. The client will see these meal slots for the selected date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Assigned Plans

Clients can see their assigned plans:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Client View" icon="eye">
    In the client app, assigned plans appear on the **Home** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Daily View" icon="calendar">
    Clients can see meal slots and food items for each day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Details" icon="info">
    Tapping on a plan shows all meal slots, food items, and nutrition information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Assignments

You can modify assigned plans:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assignment" icon="folder">
    Go to the client's profile and find the assigned plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Assignment" icon="edit">
    Tap **Edit** to modify meal slots, food items, or dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Save your changes. Updates will be reflected in the client's view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multiple Plan Assignments

Clients can have multiple diet plans assigned:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Multiple Plans" icon="plus">
    Create different diet plans for different goals or time periods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to Different Dates" icon="calendar">
    Assign each plan to non-overlapping date ranges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client Sees Active Plan" icon="eye">
    Clients will see the active plan for the current date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
You can create weekly rotation plans by assigning different plans to different days of the week.
</Callout>

## Tips for Assigning Diet Plans

<Expandable title="Best Practices" default-open="false">
- **Clear Date Ranges**: Set clear start and end dates to avoid confusion
- **Communicate Changes**: Let clients know when new plans are assigned
- **Consider Client Schedule**: Assign plans that align with the client's daily routine
- **Gradual Transitions**: When changing plans, consider overlapping dates for smooth transitions
- **Review Before Assigning**: Double-check meal slots and food items before assigning
- **Monitor Adherence**: Check how well clients are following assigned plans
- **Update as Needed**: Adjust assignments based on client progress and feedback
</Expandable>
